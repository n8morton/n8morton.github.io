About Me

My name is Nate Morton and I am a full stack web developer living in the Richmond, VA area. I moved here from New Jersey two years ago, where I worked in the casino industry for over a decade to start a family and change careers. I began studying coding as I have always had a passion for computers and app development and I recently completed a bootcamp certification program at the University of Richmond. I am proficient in the use of HTML, CSS Javascript, and Jquery for designing client side web apps as well as Node, Express, SQL, React, and Mongo for server side and database development. I have experience working with and creating Restful APIs and have included some of my recent projects in my portfolio section to showcase my work. I am always looking for new opportunities in the industry that will challegenge me to push myself and continue my learning in this ever growing field. Additionally, I always look forward to tackling interesting problems and exploring new techolgies.
